Bleed the Brake System:

Use a brake bleeder kit to remove air from the brake lines. Start with the brake farthest from the master cylinder and work your way closer. Check for Leaks: Inspect the entire brake system for any leaks that might have allowed air to enter. Repair any leaks before bleeding the system. Regular Maintenance: Periodically check and bleed the brake system as part of regular maintenance to prevent air buildup.
